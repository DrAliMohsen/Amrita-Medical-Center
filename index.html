Our Story

A Trusted Name in
Abu Dhabi Healthcare

Amrita Medical Center was founded with a singular mission: to bring the highest standards of medical practice to the communities of Abu Dhabi. Housed in a purpose-built facility in Al Khalidiya, our center combines cutting-edge diagnostics with a patient-first culture.

Our team of specialists, nurses, and support staff speak your language — literally and figuratively — offering care in Arabic, English, and several other languages to serve Abu Dhabi's diverse population.

  • MOH Licensed & Internationally Accredited
  • Multidisciplinary team of 40+ specialists
  • State-of-the-art diagnostics on-site
  • Bilingual patient support (Arabic & English)
